Installation
--------------
To install, copy the following files and directories to the webserver where the site resides:
 - api.php
 - context.php
 - handler.php
 - handlers/
   - add_feeds.php
   - authenticate.php
   - delete_feeds.php
   - edit_feeds.php
   - get_feeds.php
   - update_feeds_base.php
   - user_info.php
 - helpers/
   - error.php
   - result.php
 - objects/
   - add_feed.php
   - edit_feed.php
   - feed.php
